Ar Scáth A Chéile

"Ar Scáth A Chéile a Mhaireann na Daoine" - Irish Proverb

Framing Statement

This project aims to revitalize Irish folklore through the expressive medium of dance. By collaborating with primary school children in creative workshops, we explore how traditional stories from the Dúchas Schools Collection can be brought to life on stage.

These shadow dance performances will not only interpret folklore but also weave spoken word and visual art into immersive public experiences. The result: a living, evolving art form that honors and reanimates Ireland’s cultural memory.
